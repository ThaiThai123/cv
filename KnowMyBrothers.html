The HGP and My Brothers

The Hidden Genius Project is a program that provides male black youth with the knowledge of Computer Science, Entrepreneurship, and Leadership skills that they may not recieve anywhere else. In the Hidden Genius Project, the geniuses will build brotherhoods with the other geniuses and work together while in the program and beyond.
