About The Hidden Genius Project

The Hidden Genius Projects mission statement is "The Hidden Genius Project trains and mentors black male youth in technology creation, Entrepreneurship, and Leadership skills to transform their lives and communities" They take young black boys in so that they can be taught more skills and be given more oppourtunities that they would usually have. They exspose the Geniuses to multiple buisness which gives them the oppourtunity to both see the buinsess life/setting and meet the workers which can possibly give them the chance for internships.
